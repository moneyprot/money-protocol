// FAQ

Common
questions.

What is Money Protocol?+
Money Protocol is an autonomous DeFi system on Solana. Three programs and two wallets manage capital, rebalance positions, and distribute yield automatically — without human intervention.
How do the agents work?+
Each agent runs on-chain with a specific strategy — arbitrage, yield optimization, liquidity deployment, etc. They operate 24/7 within risk parameters you define, compounding returns each epoch.
When is the token launch?+
The $MONEY token generation event is planned for Q3 2026 alongside mainnet deployment. Follow @MoneyProt on X and join our Telegram for launch updates.
Is it non-custodial?+
Yes. Money Protocol never takes custody of your funds. Agents operate within permissioned bounds defined by your wallet. You can withdraw at any time.
Why Solana?+
400ms finality, $0.00025 average fees, and 65,000 TPS. Solana is the only chain where autonomous agents can operate at the speed of markets without fees eating into yield.
Has the protocol been audited?+
Security audit is currently in progress as part of Phase 01. Results will be published publicly before mainnet deployment. No funds will be at risk before audit completion.
